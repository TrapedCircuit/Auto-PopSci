# Transformer -- Narrative Plan

Duration: ~5 min | Audience: cs-student | Language: zh-CN | Voice: zh-CN-YunxiNeural

## Part 1: 起 -- Hook (0:00 - 0:50)

**S01_HookScene** (~50s) - ChatGPT, translation, coding -- the secret behind it all is Transformer (2017). Title reveal.

## Part 2: 承 -- Build (0:50 - 2:20)

**S02_OverviewScene** (~35s) - Encoder-decoder architecture overview. Self-attention is the key innovation.
**S03_EmbeddingScene** (~40s) - Words → vectors via embedding. Positional encoding adds order information.

## Part 3: 转 -- Deepen (2:20 - 4:00)

**S04_SelfAttentionScene** (~60s) - Context-dependent meaning ("苹果"). Q, K, V mechanism. Attention weights and weighted sum.
**S05_MultiHeadScene** (~35s) - Multiple attention heads for different relationship types. Concat + linear.

## Part 4: 合 -- Conclude (4:00 - 5:10)

**S06_TransformerBlockScene** (~30s) - Full block: attention + FFN + residual + layer norm. Stacking layers.
**S07_ApplicationScene** (~25s) - GPT, BERT, ViT -- Transformer as the universal architecture.
**S08_OutroScene** (~25s) - "Attention Is All You Need" closing.
