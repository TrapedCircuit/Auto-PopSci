# Euler's Identity -- Narrative Plan

Duration: ~5 min | Audience: cs-student | Language: zh-CN | Voice: zh-CN-YunxiNeural

## Part 1: 起 -- Hook (0:00 - 0:50)

**S01_HookScene** (~30s) - "The most beautiful equation" -- what makes an equation beautiful?
**S02_TitleScene** (~10s) - Title: e^{i*pi} + 1 = 0

## Part 2: 承 -- Build (0:50 - 2:30)

**S03_IngredientsScene** (~40s) - The five fundamental constants: e, i, pi, 1, 0
**S04_ComplexPlaneScene** (~50s) - What is i? Complex plane. Rotation interpretation.

## Part 3: 转 -- Deepen (2:30 - 4:00)

**S05_EulerFormulaScene** (~50s) - Euler's formula: e^{i*theta} = cos(theta) + i*sin(theta). Unit circle walk.
**S06_PlugInPiScene** (~30s) - Plug in theta = pi. cos(pi) = -1, sin(pi) = 0. Therefore e^{i*pi} = -1.

## Part 4: 合 -- Conclude (4:00 - 5:00)

**S07_BeautyScene** (~30s) - Why it's beautiful: five constants, three operations, one equation.
**S08_OutroScene** (~20s) - Closing.
